Campus Life

amphitheatre

An all-season and spacious Amphitheatre built in the core of the school building offers a panoramic view of the entire school premises. From the time it is roofed with a beautifully designed canopy and fitted with huge heli fans the spherically structured arena with abundant seating for students and staff is a perfect venue for celebrations, co-curricular activities and entertainment. Exquisitely landscaped with palm trees dotting the vistas, this cupola provides students a conducive milieu outside the four walls of the classrooms.

Swimming Pool

The school has a fully operational swimming pool where students go regularly and enjoy this sport. A trained coach and lifeguard are on duty throughout and utmost care is taken to maintain the cleanliness and hygiene of this area. The tiny tots have a Splash Pool where they have their rendezvous with water.

Kala Ashram

Every child is blessed with some unique talent. At Mayoor, we encourage our budding artists, dancers and musicians at the Kalashram which houses Theater, Art and Music/Dance rooms. Here creativity flourishes and self expression unleashes by stimulating the imagination of the students. Values such as team work, confidence, discipline, etc are instilled in the young minds leading to their holistic development.
